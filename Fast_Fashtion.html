The Destiny of Donated Clothings

Mothion and Animation Video

This video follows the process of donating fast fashion clothing and explains where this clothing actually ends up in the resale, recycle, disposal chain. It reveals the final destination of these products and how this impacts the environment. The goal of the video is to create consumer awareness around environmental issues associated with the purchase and disposal of fast fashion clothing.
