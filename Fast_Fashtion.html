The Destiny of Donated Clothings

Mothion and Animation Design

This is a motion video that was made with Figma to show my Figma prototype and motion skill. And this video is about what went wrong with fast fashion. I based on one of my experiences which is I bring my old clothing to the donation bin. I started with my research and found out how those donated clothing actually goes, such as resell, charities, and recycle. In the end, it shows that we still have a lot of them left with bad endings, and give the readers a suggestion.
