User Interface Deisgn

This design is a new plug-in feature for Google Meet Online Learning. Based on Google Meet captioning, the plug-in offers a new critique feature. When students upload and present their work in a class meeting room, the critique feature enables other meeting participants to click on the work and leave comments. The plug-in also has a record capability that can transcribe speech into written notes. In addition, a highlighting feature can organize notes by color coding to allow for easy information retrieval.
