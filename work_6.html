MY TRAFFIC LIFE

My data collection is on my driving traffic when I’m commuting to work and
school every week. I recorded aspects including, how the traffic is, what I do to
entertain myself and how I’m feeling when I am driving. Every week, I spend a
very long time driving.

Details

time

1 unit is 1 hour

traffic level

the thicker line, the worse traffic

feeling

different color stand for different feeling,
BLUE is relax; YELLOW is nervous; PINK is tired; GREEN is zone out;

entertainments

star is for listening news; circle is for music; ellipse is for comedy;

derection

going inbound if the shape is on the left; going outbound if the shape is on the right;
