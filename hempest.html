The Hempest ReBrand

Hempest first opened in 1995 and is oldest cannabis shop in Massachusetts. It specializes in hemp clothing, accessories, and CBD products. With state legalization of cannabis, the popularity of hemp, CBD and cannabis products is increasing. The goal of this rebrand is to give the store a more contemporary retail presence that will appeal to a wider customer audience. The icon design uses cannabis leaves to form an H monogram.
