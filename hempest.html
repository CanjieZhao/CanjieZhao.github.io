The Hempest ReBrand

The HEMPEST is a rebrand project for a local store. They sell clothing that make from hemp, CBD products, and other accessories. I used two low saturation Greens, and a type of gothic typeface to create the logo for its main color and typeface. The low saturation green is avoiding the dirty feeling. Used an extended type and increased its tracking in order to let it loose and relax.
